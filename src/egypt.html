trip to the pyramids in Egypt

The three pyramids of Egypt are characterized as one of the most important of the Seven Wonders of the Republic of Egypt. They are located on the Giza Plateau in the Giza Governorate, also located on the western bank of the Nile. They include three pyramids: Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure, which were built around 25 centuries BC.

and They are royal tombs, each bearing the name of the king who built it and who was buried in it. The best activities that you can do on this trip: You can enjoy a camel ride, as you can rent a camel and take a tour around these pyramids to see their hugeness and the grandeur of building these pyramids thousands of years ago, and you can take the most beautiful memorial photos there.
