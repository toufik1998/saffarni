Trip to Istanbul, Turkey.

Istanbul is one of the largest and most beautiful cities of Turkey and is classified among the most attractive tourist cities in the world. Istanbul is the first choice for tourists who are thinking of going to Turkey. Our tour will be in the following attractions

1. Hagia Sophia Museum: It was known in the Ottoman era as the Grand Mosque of Hagia Sophia. It is located on the European bank in Istanbul and is one of the most famous landmarks in Turkey that everyone is keen to visit during tourism tours in Istanbul.

2. Topkapi Palace: One of the best tourist places in Istanbul, it is recommended to visit it to find out how the affairs of life and governance were managed under the Ottoman Caliphate in Turkey for 4 centuries. The great museum reviews its events and details.

3. Galata Tower: Galata Tower is an ancient historical tower that is said to have been built in 507 AD, then the tower was subjected to a strong earthquake that had a great impact on it, after which it was restored and built in the Romanesque style in the 14th century, and at this time it was the tallest building in Istanbul.

4. Eyup Sultan Mosque: An old Ottoman mosque located in the Eyup area on the European side of Istanbul, near the Golden Horn. It is located outside the walls of Constantinople. The mosque was built in 1458, five years after the conquest of Constantinople. Istanbul after the conquest of Constantinople in 1453.
